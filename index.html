The Chronicles of the Mark

The Genesis of Crownhaven

Before the Empire, there was only the Republic—a defiant City-State forged upon the central plains. To the North and East, the cruel seas and harsh tides lashed the coast. To the West, the scorching deserts and choking Mangrove swamps stood sentinel. Crownhaven stood alone, a beacon of civilization carved out of a hostile world.

The Fall of Ashwell

Commerce was the first weapon of war. The rival city of Ashwell attempted to starve Crownhaven of trade, luring merchants with "Free Market" stalls. But Ashwell’s gold was tainted by tyranny. When Crownhaven exposed the iron-fisted governance of their rival, the people fled the shadows of Ashwell for the light of the Republic. In a final masterstroke, Crownhaven simply bought the city outright, absorbing its legacy and transforming the Republic into a Kingdom.

The Great Forest Blight

As the Kingdom hungered for the Southlands, a pioneer named Jack struck a bargain with the Crown. To tame the untamed East and South, Jack’s Landing was founded. What began as a strategic outpost evolved into a neon-lit den of decadence. To satisfy the insatiable demand for entertainment, the Great Forests of the Southland were put to the torch—the skies turned black as ancient trees were torn to shreds to pave the way for Casinos and Jousting arenas.

The Imperial Ascension

The Kingdom was no longer sufficient for the scale of such ambition. From the ashes of the Southlands, the **Empire of Crownveil** was proclaimed. The chaotic whims of the past were replaced by a Formal Legal Code, and the **Imperial Mark** became the heartbeat of global trade. Order has been established. All is recorded.
