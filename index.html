The Chronicles of the Mark

I. THE GENESIS OF CROWNHAVEN

Before the Empire, there was only the Republic—a defiant City-State forged upon the central plains. To the North and East, the cruel seas lashed the coast; to the West, scorching deserts and Mangrove swamps stood sentinel. Crownhaven stood as a lone beacon of order carved out of a hostile world.

II. THE FALL OF ASHWELL

Commerce was the first weapon of war. The rival city of Ashwell attempted to starve Crownhaven of trade with market stalls, but their governance was iron-fisted and cruel. When Crownhaven exposed the truth, the population fled to the Republic. Crownhaven bought the city outright, absorbing its legacy and transforming the Republic into a Kingdom.

III. THE FOREST BLIGHT

A pioneer named Jack struck a bargain with the Crown to tame the Southlands. Jack’s Landing began as an outpost but evolved into a den of decadence. To satisfy the demand for entertainment, the Great Forests were put to the torch; ancient trees were torn to shreds to pave the way for Casinos and Jousting arenas.

IV. THE ASCENSION

The Kingdom evolved into the Empire of Crownveil. Chaos was replaced by the Formal Legal Code, and the Imperial Mark became the heartbeat of global trade. Order has been established. All is recorded.
